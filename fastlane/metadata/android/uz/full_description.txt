Ushbu Muqaddas Kitob aplikatsiyasi ochib manbalarga asoslangan bo'lib, uni internetsiz o'qish uchun yuklab olsangiz bo'ladi. Uni bepul va hech qanaqa reklamalarsiz tagdim etyapmiz, zero niyatimiz barcha davlatlarda odamlar Muqaddas Kitobni qiyinchiliksiz o'qib, eshitib va mutolaa qilib ulkan marhamatga ega bo'lsinlar.

Muqaddas Kitob, Sharhlar, Teologik Lug'atlar, Haritalar, va 700 tillardagi Masihiylik haqidagi kitoblar.
Yulab olingandan so'ng, barcha fayllarni, hamda qidiruv, eslatmalar, sharhlar va shunga o'xshaganlarni internetsiz ishlatsa bo'ladi.
* Interface in multiple languages

The provenance of all documents is thoroughly researched to ensure all documents can be legally distributed or permission has been granted by the copyright holder.  That is also the reason And Bible can not contain documents like NIV and NKJV.
